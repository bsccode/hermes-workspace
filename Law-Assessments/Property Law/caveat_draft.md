Statement of Interest
The Caveator claims an equitable interest in the land described above arising from a contract for the sale of the land between the Caveator (Purchaser) and the registered proprietor Gideon Sterling (Vendor) dated 15 April 2026 (the Contract). Under the Contract, the Purchaser agreed to purchase the property for $1,200,000.00 and has paid a deposit of $120,000.00. Settlement was scheduled for 28 days after signing (13 May 2026).

The Caveator’s equitable interest arises because the Contract is a binding agreement for the disposition of land and the Caveator has performed her obligations (or remains ready and willing to perform subject to the contract conditions) and has an interest that would be defeated by registration of adverse dealings by the Vendor without notice to the Caveator.

Relief sought
The Caveator seeks to prevent registration of any dealing that would adversely affect the Caveator’s equitable interest, pending resolution of the dispute between the parties or until the Caveator consents in writing.
